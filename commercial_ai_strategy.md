# AI Integration Strategy for Commercial BU: The RM3M Blueprint

**Objective:** Drive Productivity, Reduce Manual Work, Bring Value to Customer, and Accelerate to the RM3M Target

---

## 1. Executive Summary: Aligning AI with BU Goals
Integrating AI into our Account Management (AM) workflow is a strategic move to directly support our core OKRs and hit the RM3M revenue target. We are shifting the AM role from an administrative burden to a high-efficiency closing machine by automating the entire sales lifecycle.

By implementing these consolidated AI use cases, we will:
*   **Improve Productivity:** Free AMs from research, drafting, and data entry.
*   **Reduce Manual Work:** Eliminate manual cross-checking, pipeline tracking, and CRM logging.
*   **Bring Value to Customer:** Deliver faster response times, highly personalized pitches, and structured ROI justifications.
*   **Help BU Achieve OKR/PKR:** Accelerate lead qualification, increase close rates, and drastically reduce the time-to-proposal.
*   **Elevate Self Reputation & Own Branding:** AMs stop doing mundane data entry and start delivering strategic market value to their clients and managers.

---

## 2. Low-Value Daily Routines to be Replaced by AI
Before executing a high-level strategy, we will immediately use AI to replace the following repetitive, low-value tasks that drain AM productivity:
*   Lead research and competitor comparison
*   Pre-meeting agenda preparation
*   Proposal first drafts and pricing comparisons
*   Writing repetitive follow-up messages and email drafting
*   Call summaries and CRM note-taking
*   Sales reporting

## 4. Anticipated Risks & Mitigation Strategies

To ensure successful deployment, we will enforce strict mitigation protocols:

1.  **"Garbage In, Garbage Out" (Proposals & Pitches)**
    *   **Risk:** AI generates inaccurate ROI or hallucinates product features if left unchecked.
    *   **Mitigation:** AI is strictly locked to approved templates and pricing guardrails. A human AM must review and click "Approve" before any AI document or email is sent.
2.  **Losing the Human Element (Off-boarding & Collections)**
    *   **Risk:** Tone-deaf automated collections or reassigning accounts without understanding relationship nuances.
    *   **Mitigation:** AI acts as a recommendation engine. Managers make final account assignments. Furthermore, AMs have a mandatory "Kill Switch" to cancel any automated payment reminder if the client relationship requires a delicate touch.
